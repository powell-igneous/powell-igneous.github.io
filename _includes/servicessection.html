A full range of engineering and surveying services

  • Surveying

    Are you looking to find your property lines to build a fence, or do you want to construct a new house or addition? We can help with that. Do you want to purchase a beautiful parcel of ground that you can call all your own? We can assist you by finding your property boundary lines and corners and preparing the legal descriptions.

  • Civil Engineering

    As architects and developers know, it all starts with a base drawing of the existing site topography, property lines, and utilities. These services are used in the proper planning and design that result in a successful project. We understand that and can help you through the process.

  • Environmental Engineering

    You don’t want to buy an environmental problem that you didn’t know about but are now responsible for. A Phase I Environmental Site Assessment will explore the site’s history and reveal potential problems. Need a few monitoring wells installed or sampled? Do you have some routine water sampling or testing required to maintain your permit? We offer all of these types of services.
